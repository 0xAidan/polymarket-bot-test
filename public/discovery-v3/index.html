Discovery v3

Tiered wallet discovery with point-in-time purity

Alpha

Wallets that enter markets before the price moves. They have a high win rate and their trades tend to predict which way a market will resolve. Best for news-driven events.

  • High CLV — they buy low before consensus forms
  • Strong Brier score — their probability estimates are accurate
  • Early entry on major markets
Whale

High-volume traders with a proven profitability track record. They trade large amounts with conviction and their entries can move markets. Best for copying size and direction.

  • Large average trade size ($1K+)
  • Consistent positive realized PnL over 90+ days
  • Trades are liquid enough to replicate
Specialist

Experts in a specific category — politics, crypto, sports, macro. They outperform in their niche because they know context that the broader market misses.

  • High niche concentration (≥60% volume in one category)
  • Edge rate above baseline in their category
  • Consistent wins within their domain
