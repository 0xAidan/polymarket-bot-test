Tier guide

Alpha

Wallets that enter markets before the price moves. Their trades predict resolution direction. High win rate, strong Brier calibration, and positive closing-line value.

  • CLV > 0 — buying early before consensus forms
  • Brier score below population median
  • Documented early entry on large markets
Whale

High-volume traders with consistent profitability over 90+ days. Their size convictions can move prices. Best for copying direction and position sizing.

  • Average trade size $1 K+, replicable liquidity
  • Positive realized PnL across 90-day window
  • Consistent hit rate across diverse markets
Specialist

Domain experts in one category — politics, crypto, sports, macro. They outperform in their niche through context the broader market lacks.

  • ≥ 60 % of volume concentrated in one category
  • Edge rate above category baseline
  • Consistent wins within their domain
